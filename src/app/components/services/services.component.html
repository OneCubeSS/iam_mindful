Learning Disability, Identification and Remediation

Children with learning disabilities may have difficulty in reading, writing, spelling and reasoning, it cannot be solved if we continue to teach through traditional teaching methods. For teaching such children, specially trained teachers are required. However, the number of trained teachers in this area are very less. Hence, I Am Mindful aims to produce specially trained teachers and resources through our Certificate course in Learning Disability.

For Teachers already in schools this course will help them identify the children during the early stages, provide inclusive learning and remediation or redirect them to the right resources to address them.
